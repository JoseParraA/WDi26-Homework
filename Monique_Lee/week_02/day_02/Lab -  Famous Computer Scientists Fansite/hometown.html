Hometown

Matsumoto was born in Osaka Prefecture which is located in the Kansai region on Honshu, the main island of Japan. He grew up in Tottori Prefecture.
sources: Wikipedia

Tottori is a coastal prefecture in southern Honshu, Japan's main island. It is the least populated prefecture in Japan. To the east lie the Tottori Sand Dunes, the largest sand hills in Japan.
