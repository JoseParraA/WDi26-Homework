Biography

Yukihiro Matsumoto is a super famous computer scientst from Japan. He was born 14 April 1965 and is best known for his work as the chief designer of the Ruby programming language, which he named on 24 February 1993 and released to the internet on 21 December 1995.
Matsumoto has an information science degree from the University of Tukuba. Matsumoto worked at Heroku in San Francisco, which was one of the first cloud platforms. He is a fellow of the NaCl - Open Source Frontier - and also of the Rakuten Institute of Technology.
