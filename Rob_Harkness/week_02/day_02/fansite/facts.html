John von Neumann - Quick Maths (fun facts)

John von Neumann was some sort of mathematical super being, he could instantaneously perform complex operations in his head such as summing geometric series. He also had a remarkable eidetic (photographic) memory and would entertain friends by memorising pages of a telephone directory and reciting names, addresses and numbers from randomly provided page numbers.

Von Neumann worked extensively on the Manhattan project developing the atomic bomb for the United States and was heavily instrumental in brining about the state of "assured mutual destruction" during the cold war with Russia.
