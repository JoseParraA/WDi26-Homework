Jenn Schiffer started as a Computer Science major at Montclair State University. After graduating and receiving her bachelor’s for Computer Science in 2007, she went for her for a Master’s Degree in Computer the following school year. During her Master’s program, Schiffer managed to get experience as a Department Administrator, for computer science, in July 2011 till May 2013.

In September 2009 till May 2011, Schiffer taught her university’s Introduction to Computer Application/Fluency with Information Technology, she helped professors and her fellow students in Foundations of Computer Science labs, and trained graduate assistants in administration of the Computer Science department website.

Currently, she has mentioned that she would not be where she is today as a programmer and artist if it wasn’t for a project she started around 2016 called make8bitart.com. Make8bitart.com is an open source in-browser application built to draw pixel art and has so far at the end of 2017, managed to have 348,071 enter the site and 1,318 days and 12 hours total time spent by all users.

A few more work experiences she has includes being a Senior Front-End Web Developer for the NBA for a year, worked for Bocoup as an Open Web Engineer for roughly 3 years, and is currently working for Fog Creek Software as a Community Engineer.
